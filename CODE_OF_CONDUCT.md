# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR
Be kind. Be respectful. Assume good intent. Keep it constructive.

This project is maintained by humans. We welcome contributions, questions, and feedback.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate in tone and content
- Welcoming different viewpoints and levels of experience
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or hostile behavior
- Publishing private information without explicit permission

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community channels used for the project).

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communications that violate this Code of Conduct.
